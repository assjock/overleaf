\iffalse

 \qquad 由Cauchy不等式得\\$(n^2+(n-1)^2+(n-2)^2…1^2+0^2)(a_1^2+(a_1-a_2)^2+(a_2-a_3)^2+…+a_n^2) \\ \geq (na_1^2-(n-1)a_1^2+(n-1)a_2^2-(n-2)a_2^2+(n-2)a_3^2-(n-3)a_3^2+…+a_n^2)^2$\\
